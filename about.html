About Me

I'm @thekzbn, an independent researcher dedicated to rigorous inquiry across multiple disciplines. My work spans educational policy, technology studies, social sciences, and emerging interdisciplinary fields.

Rather than confining myself to a single academic domain, I embrace methodological pluralism and interdisciplinary approaches. This site serves as my digital repository for peer-reviewed papers, working papers, research notes, and scholarly reflections.

My research philosophy centers on the belief that complex contemporary challenges require diverse perspectives and rigorous analytical frameworks. I'm particularly interested in how traditional academic boundaries can be productively crossed to generate new insights.
